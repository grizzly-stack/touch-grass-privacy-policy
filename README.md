# touch-grass-privacy-policy
privacy policy for touch grass app

**Last updated: March 2026**

Touch Grass ("the App") is designed with your privacy as a priority. This policy explains how the App handles your information. By using the App, you agree to the practices described below.

---

## Data Stored Locally

All data — including screen time statistics, plant progress, streaks, seed balances, app preferences, and blocking schedules — is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's on-device frameworks.

- No databases, cloud services, or remote servers are used to store your data.
- Any optional Sign in with Apple information (such as name or email) is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

---

## No Data Collected

Touch Grass does **not collect, upload, or transmit any personal data**.

- No personal data is sent to the developer
- No usage analytics are collected
- No crash reporting services are used
- No advertising SDKs are used

All in-app purchases are processed securely by Apple through the App Store. The developer does not receive or store any payment information.

---

## No Tracking

Touch Grass does not track you.

- No cross-app or cross-website tracking
- No use of advertising identifiers (IDFA)
- No fingerprinting or tracking technologies
- No participation in ad networks or data broker programs

App Tracking Transparency status: **Tracking Not Used**

---

## Screen Time Usage

Touch Grass uses Apple’s Screen Time APIs (FamilyControls and DeviceActivity frameworks) to help users manage app usage.

- Screen Time data is processed entirely on-device
- The developer does not have access to this data
- Data is used only to:
  - display usage statistics
  - calculate averages and streaks
  - manage app blocking schedules

Screen Time permissions can be revoked at any time in:

**Settings → Screen Time**

---

## Data Deletion

You have full control over your data.

- Deleting the app removes all stored data
- Data is stored only on-device, so deletion is immediate
- Screen Time permissions can be revoked at any time in iOS Settings

---

## Children's Privacy

Touch Grass does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

- The app does not require an account to function

---

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.

Any updates will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
